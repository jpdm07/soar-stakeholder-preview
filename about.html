About Sierra Overlook Animal Rescue

Founded in Somerset, California, SOAR is a sanctuary-based rescue for animals who are elderly, medically complex, behaviorally challenging, or otherwise overlooked—paired with humane education for rural communities. We’re the place that says “we’ve got you” when nowhere else could.

Mission & vision

We believe every animal deserves compassion, individualized care, and—when release or adoption is not possible—a dignified forever home on sanctuary grounds.
