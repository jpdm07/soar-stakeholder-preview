Our mission

SOAR provides a safe, loving sanctuary for animals who have been abused, abandoned, or can no longer survive in the wild—paired with education and advocacy that deepens the bond between people, animals, and the land we share. When you give, volunteer, or shop the store, you’re telling them they still matter.
